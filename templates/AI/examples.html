7 examples of Artificial Intelligence which impacts your daily life!

1. Netflix or Spotify: Do you ever wonder how the system is able to provide you with recommendations based of your preferences? this is because of Artificial Intelligence! they use a weak AI that collects the data on your viewer history and provides you with recommendations towards the similar genre or artist/actor allowing you to explore more towards your preferred genre.

2. Siri: Siri is a weak AI which uses Natural Language Processing & Voice recognition which allows Siri to recognize the voice in speech form and respond accordingly with directions, research, functions etc.This allows the user to access all kinds of functions in their device using Voice recognition.

3. Self Driven transportation: If you live in a city that runs with self driven transportation you'd know that this plays a significant role on how on how the city runs, this is an example of strong AI. the train tracks normally consist of wires which sends signals to and from the train allowing the system to recognize the distance between the train in front of it or how far away is the next station allowing the train to slow down.

4. Web search: We use web search in our everyday life either for researching purposes or trying to access another website. this is an example of weak AI. The user inputs the data which sends a request to the web server and file which seeks the input information to databases and returns back a document to the user.

5. Traffic lights: traffic lights uses Artificial Intelligence algorithm, this is an example of weak AI. the artificial intelligence algorithm allows traffic lights to deal with traffic jams due to Machine Learning.. The algorithm allows the traffic signals to coordinate in intersections.

6. Google maps: Google maps is an example of a weak AI. Google maps uses Machine Learning allows the app to recognize the visuals of buildings and streets.

7. Video games: Artificial Intelligence in video games are used to generate movement and behavior for NPC's(Non-player characters). this allows the NPC's to serve its purpose depending on what it was assigned to do. this can be called as Strong AI as its purpose it to accomplish tasks a human being can.

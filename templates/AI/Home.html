Python in Artificial Intelligence: What does the future hold?

By Shobhit Chaudhary

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Before getting into the complexity of artificial intelligence it is important we understand what it is first, Artificial intelligence is intelligence displayed by machines such as phones, Robots, computers etc. these are usually programmed using high level languages. Artificial Intelligence can observe its environment and act upon, learn and solve problems. These can be narrowed down into Weak AI and Strong AI.

Weak AI: Weak AI can only be used to solve minor specific tasks, examples of these can be computer chess players, social media, music apps such as Spotify which recommends you music to listen to, keep your email mailbox free of spam etc.

Strong AI: Also known as General AI, Strong AI is the intelligence of computer which can be programmed to solve tasks like a human being can, this is harder to create and is the way the future is heading, the future is being planned to run mainly by Strong AI.

How Does Artificial Intelligence Work?

Artificial Intelligence works by collecting a significant amount of Data regarding its surroundings and the functions for it to act upon its surroundings by the algorithm these can be broken down into multiple applications:

Machine Learning: Also known as ML is the most common Application of Artificial Intelligence and is most likely the first thing that comes up in your head when you think of Artificial Intelligence, this however is not only Robots. But is also the ability of allowing programs to automatically learn and improve by collecting data from experience without being explicitly programmed to do so, this is a result from an algorithm programmed and analyze data allowing the AI to make predictions/decisions based on the data provided, similarly this is exactly how most applications such as Netflix are functioned, they are manufactured using Machine Learning and collect data from experience allowing the application to act accordingly by recommending you similar movies based on your interests.

Deep Learning: Deep learning is a subset of Machine Learning and is mostly how Robots are functioned, they process data provided to them and act upon it. Deep Learning acts similarly to the Human Brain, it processes the information provided by the "six senses". Deep Learning consists of neural networks which functions similar to neurons of the human brain.

Cognitive Computing: Cognitive Computing is also a vital Artificial Intelligence component, especially when it comes to programming Robots for the future as its set to have constant human to machine interaction. this is processed by understanding human languages.

Natural Language Processing: Natural Language Processing helps the AI understand Human Language in speech form, as mentioned above for Cognitive Computing the main goal of NLP is to have a fully functioning robot to human interaction which can happen by programming the AI to produce logical responses.

Computer Vision: Computer vision is the implantation of Deep Learning and pattern identification to recognize content of images and diagrams, this allows the AI to process visual data and act within it, an example of this is x-ray machines or Covid-19 temperature checks.

How is Artificial Intelligence going to be used in the future? what does the future bring?

Artificial Intelligence is soon going to take over every industry and have a major impact on global context, this include self controlled Robots whom will mainly work under Natural Language Processing and Cognitive Computer. The future brings more human to robot interaction providing more digital services, and technical innovation by replacing human workers with Robots. this shows that not only will many jobs no longer exist there will also be many new jobs coming to the field.

however, Robots aren't the only thing the future holds! Game development will have a major leap when it comes to Artificial Intelligence Involvement, the gaming industry is set to have more Virtual Reality influences according to game professionals.

How does Python links with Artificial Intelligence?

Python is a high level programming language which is also easy to learn. Python also has pre built libraries such as Pybrain in it which allows advanced computing and can be used in Robots or Machine learning. Not only can Python be used for Machine Learning but can also be used for scientific computation.
